About Architecture courses

Architecture courses deal with the process and product of planning, designing, and constructing buildings or any other structures. In India, Bachelor of Architecture lasts for five years. Specialised institutes founded by the government of India, for excellence in the field of architecture and planning are SPAs (Schools of Planning and Architecture)
